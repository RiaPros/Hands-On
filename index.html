Who We Are

We are a student-run STEM outreach society at the University of Edinburgh. We started out in 2021 looking to fill a gap in EUSA's socieities - there were plenty of STEM societies, and a few volunteering ones too. Then we came along! If you are interested in promoting STEM to local children in the Edinburgh area then we are the society for you!

Our Mission

We design and deliver STEM outreach activities to different youth groups such as school or scouts across Edinburgh and Midlothian area in hope to inspire the next generation and encourage equality and diversity in the STEM community.
